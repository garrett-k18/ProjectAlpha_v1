ProjectAlpha Color Palette Reference
=====================================
Professional Navy & Gold Theme for Investment Platform

Last Updated: December 2024


PRIMARY PALETTE
===============

Primary Navy: #1B3B5F
Description: Main brand color, used for sidebar, headers, primary text
Usage: Sidebar background, main navigation, primary headings

Accent Gold: #D4AF37
Description: Premium accent color for calls-to-action and highlights
Usage: Primary buttons, active states, important highlights, key metrics


SECONDARY & ACCENT COLORS
==========================

ACCESSIBILITY NOTES
===================

- All color combinations should meet WCAG 2.1 AA standards (4.5:1 contrast ratio for normal text)
- Navy (#1B3B5F) on White (#FFFFFF): 9.8:1 ✓
- Charcoal (#2C3E50) on White (#FFFFFF): 8.6:1 ✓
- Gold (#D4AF37) on Navy (#1B3B5F): 4.6:1 ✓
- Always test color combinations for accessibility
- Provide non-color indicators (icons, text labels) alongside color coding for status
- Consider color-blind users: avoid red/green as sole differentiators
